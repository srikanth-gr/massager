# Project Requirements: Shoulder Massage Tennis Ball Holder

## 1. Functional Objective
Design an industrial-strength, ergonomic shoulder massage tool holding a standard tennis ball. The device is mechanically partitioned to isolate lateral retaining forces from heavy vertical body-weight compression forces. The back of the primary foundation plate is completely flat to lay flush against a wall or floor during use.

## 2. Mechanical Design & Assembly Profile
- **Solid Continuous Base:** The base is a solid, continuous $15\text{mm}$ thick disc with no internal deep pockets, providing uniform tensile strength.
- **2x Scaled Raised Pedestal Support:** A solid $24\text{mm} \times 32\text{mm} \times 12\text{mm}$ housing block is fused directly onto the top face of the base, keeping structural wall thicknesses twice the wheel radius scale.
- **Standalone Roller Wheel:** A separate $12\text{mm}$ diameter, $16\text{mm}$ wide tangent wheel is pinned horizontally along the Y-axis inside the pedestal slot via a $4\text{mm}$ filament pin or metal rod to support smooth forward/backward ball rotation.
- **Squat Sturdy Support Arms:** The arms are cut down to a squat height of $68\text{mm}$, placing the top surfaces flush with the crest of the ball cups to eliminate bending deflection under load.
- **Matched Curvatures:** The outer arm profiles match the base circular footprint ($R=60\text{mm}$). The front profile of the arms is cut with a matching arc profile to seamlessly cradle the rotating cups.

## 3. Ball Cup Holders Specification
- **Ellipsoid Outer Geometry:** The outer profile of each cup is shaped like an organic ellipsoid, swelling in thickness around the equator line for maximum structural resistance against lateral pop-out forces, while tapering smoothly at the poles.
- **Calibrated Snap-Fit Opening:** The clearance passage between the faces of the cup holder borders is exactly $64\text{mm}$, creating a tactile mechanical snap fit when a standard $67\text{mm}$ tennis ball is inserted.
- **Fingerprint Texture Profile:** The inner concave spherical surface ($R=33.5\text{mm}$) is patterned with concentric, raised fingerprint-like friction ridges to grip the ball fuzz tightly.
- **Ergonomic Borders:** All outer rims are smooth and rounded to ensure comfortable handling during manual adjustments.

## 4. Structural Dimensions
- **Base Footprint:** Cylinder, $120\text{mm}$ diameter ($60\text{mm}$ radius), $15\text{mm}$ thickness.
- **Support Arms:** $22\text{mm}$ thick, $40\text{mm}$ deep, $68\text{mm}$ high.
- **Ellipsoid Cups:** $72\text{mm}$ major diameter, $25\text{mm}$ absolute base thickness, $33.5\text{mm}$ inner pocket radius.
- **Integrated Axle Stubs:** $8\text{mm}$ diameter ($4\text{mm}$ radius), $15\text{mm}$ length, fitting within $18\text{mm}$ blind pockets on the inner arms.
